#ScenTrees.jl

Warning
`ScenTrees.jl` is still under development.

ScenTrees.jl is a package for generating and improving scenario trees and scenario lattices for multistage stochastic optimization using stochastic approximation. In this explanation, we assume that the reader is quite familiar with the theory and explanation in the paper Dynamic Genaration of Scenario Trees by Pflug and Pichler.

Tip
If you haven't, you don't have to worry. Here is the link:
[Dynamic Generation of Scenario Trees](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10589-015-9758-0)
